PocketBook Pro 902 Description

The PocketBook Pro 902 supports PDF files, EPUB files and DJVU files. It supports PRC files and FB2 files. It has 200 pixels per inch and an E-ink display and displays in greyscale. It has USB connectivity, SD support and WiFi capability. The PocketBook Pro 902 has Bluetooth. It has text-to-speech functionality, notetaking/hilighting/underlining ability and search functionality. It has a dictionary. It is moderately priced. The PocketBook Pro 902 has 1 gigabyte of memory. It has a 9.7-inch screen and is 7.50 inches wide.

Many books are available for free in .txt, .pdf, and .epub formats, and these formats are supported by most e-readers. The number of e-books that are available to read on an e-reader depends largely on the number of formats that are supported by the e-reader. The most popular open formats are PDF and EPUB, both of which are formats available for download from Google Books.
